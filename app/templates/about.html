You can find the detail of her information on the footer of this website.

02. The Objective

This website is a medium for predicting and classifying WQI with optimal accuracy using the LGBM algorithm combined with RFE, SMOTE, and Optuna.

03. How to use

  • Collect data numeric on substances or microorganisms that exist in water, namely aluminium, ammonia, arsenic, bacteria, barium, cadmium, chloramine, chromium, copper, lead, nitrates, nitrites, perchlorate, radium, selenium, silver, uranium, and viruses.
  • Go to page classification and input the data in the form. Make sure you input the numeric data.
  • After you finished, click the “classify” button. This button will predict and classify your data.
  • The result will be shown on the classification results table.
  • If the result is “safe” the water is safe for drinking and other purposes. But if the results is “not safe” the water is not safe to drink or other purposes.
